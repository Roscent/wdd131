Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our shipping partner's website to track your package.

What is your return policy?+

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for a full refund. The artwork must be in its original condition and packaging. Please contact us to initiate a return.

How do I become a featured artist?+

Submit an application through our For Artists page. Our curation team reviews all applications monthly.
